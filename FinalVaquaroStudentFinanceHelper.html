Financial tips

Try the 50/30/20 rule
Put 50% to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings every paycheck.
Pay yourself first
Move your savings to a separate spot right after you get paid — before spending anything.
Make "subscriptions" visible
List every recurring charge. Cancel anything you haven't used in 30 days.
Emergency fund first
Before any other goal, try to save $500. It prevents most financial crises.
Avoid lifestyle inflation
When your income goes up, keep your expenses steady and save the difference.
